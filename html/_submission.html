Submission

We accept only electronic submissions from the workshop homepage via EasyChair. To be considered for review, a paper submission must conform, at time of submission, to the IEEE Formatting Guidelines (see ICSE 2013 Submission Guidelines). Papers must not exceed 4 pages for a short paper or 8 pages for a full paper. For accepted papers, at least one author must participate at the workshop and register for the workshop at the ICSE 2013 conference.

A link to our EasyChair page will be announced here in time.
